# LinkedIn Article Draft: AI Modernization Starts Before the Chatbot

Many enterprise AI programs start with a chatbot demo. The demo may impress stakeholders, but it often exposes the real problem: the enterprise is not yet AI-ready.

The hard work is not only model selection. It is connecting legacy systems, creating trusted data products, enforcing policy, making retrieval auditable, and operating AI services like real enterprise products.

A practical modernization path looks like this:

1. Capture high-value operational changes from ERP and core platforms.
2. Publish those changes through an event backbone.
3. Use stream processing to clean, enrich, and govern data.
4. Build lakehouse data products that business teams can trust.
5. Generate embeddings with lineage and access metadata.
6. Serve LLM applications through a governed gateway.
7. Measure quality, cost, adoption, and risk continuously.

The organizations that win with AI will not be the ones with the most pilots. They will be the ones that turn AI into a repeatable operating capability.
